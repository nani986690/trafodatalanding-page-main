ABOUT US

Traf-O-Data Analytical Solutions is a modern traffic data and mobility insights company built to help transport teams make smarter decisions using real world movement data. We turn traffic videos and field observations into clean, accurate, and actionable reports.

Our goal is simple: deliver reliable traffic counts and analytics with high accuracy, fast delivery, and clear reporting. We support projects like intersection studies, road volume surveys, pedestrian flow analysis, parking studies, and more.

With a quality-first mindset and a strong review process, we ensure every dataset is consistent and easy to understand. We focus on giving engineers, planners, and consultants the exact numbers they need to plan safer roads and better mobility systems.

At Traf-O-Data Analytical Solutions, we believe great results come from a mix of smart tools, skilled analysts, and smooth communication. From small studies to large-scale projects, we work with dedication and deliver with confidence.
